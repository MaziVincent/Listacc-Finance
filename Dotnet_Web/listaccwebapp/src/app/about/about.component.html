Who We Are

Listacc aims to provide solutions that meet international standards to individuals and businesses, putting in mind their respective customers. We are out to prove that solutions from inside Africa can be the best in class worldwide

Our Vision

We pursue a global vision to put technological solutions in the hands of every professional individual, businesses, organisation and contributing to solve social problems.

Our Mission

Staying true and dedicated to improving lives and ways of living through the application of leading edge technology in providing efficient solutions to the daily challenges of people in a timely manner.

Our Culture

At Listacc, our culture is not a reflection of one person’s personal views or behaviour.It is a house full of colors, with each shade and nuance a testimony to our diversity, but which in its entirety synthesizes into beautiful oneness. All members of the team have a considerable level of autonomy in making decisions while being accountable to superior authority. This system works because we maintain an atmosphere of trust. We trust each other’s skills, character, and capacity to grow. When a new team member joins, it becomes everyone’s duty to assimilate him/her into the family. It is called the Listacc family. Because we believe that everyone and anyone can supply great ideas, we meet thrice a week to discuss the challenges each person or team is currently facing in executing a task. Looking at problems from other angles and rubbing minds with other people in other departments has led to to several eureka moments. Because we believe in ideas, we keep them in constant flux among us. Beyond the constant exchange of ideas among us, we close each week with sharing sessions where a person stands before other members of the team and shares ideas or stories of her choice. We are always proactive. At the organizational level, we do not wait for the government or any organization to engage us. Instead, we take an active role in sourcing for problems and fixing them. Our probes are out there, feeling for problems worthy of attention. And we do not just use technology to fix other people’s problems, we do the same for the problems we face everyday. Two good examples are the Listacc Finance Suite, a bespoke software for managing all our financial activities, and TestTaker, a test-taking app.

Our Story

Putting solving Africa’s biggest challenges first, two award winning Computer science graduates from Covenant University came together and decided to take on the challenge of creating global standard technological solutions to meet Africa’s needs. We have a growing team with the drive to succeed, this is just the beginning, we are yet to scratch the surface of our vision. We will not stop until we see our solutions first-in-line solving Africa’s problems.
