Agozie Okechukwu

Chief Executive Officer

Agozie Is one of the founders of Listacc. As CEO, his responsibilities include the overall management of Listacc’s people and assets, guiding and shaping the vision of the company, and making sure things get done effectively. He devotes much of his time to mentoring members of the team, encouraging them to stretch themselves beyond their perceived limits. He remains an active developer even with his busy schedule.

                   

Olisaemeka Chukwuebuka

Chief Technical Officer

As the Chief Technological Officer of the Listacc company, Ebuka is in charge of the Dev-Ops team. His major duty is to lead the engineering team through planning and execution in order to deliver thoroughly engineered products. His emphasis is always on the details and speed. Being co-founder of Listacc alongside Agozie, Ebuka has been here since the beginning. A software engineer with over six years of experience with different technologies, he brings breath, depth, and energy to the team. Originally from ____, Ebuka graduated from the Computer Science department of Covenant University. A winner of several awards in competitive programming, he finished as the best student in his graduating set.

                   

Mazi Vincent Esomchi

Head, Training and Network Engineering

As the head of LICTA, Vincent is first a teacher and a mentor. He has taken hundreds of people through both basic and advanced ICT courses. He is responsible for planning and organizing training sessions, bootcamps, and seminars; setting up ICT centres for clients; and superintending over all the activities of our ICT centres. A graduate of Computer Science department of the renowned University of Nigeria, Nsukka, Vincent has a good background in networking computer systems. His core skills include computer programming and Systems Networking. His expertise has been the backbone behind all Listacc’s ICT centre setups and maintenance.

                   

Uchechi Okah

Product Manager, SchoolCater

SchoolCater is Listacc’s flagship product, and it is Uchechi’s duty to manage it. She reports directly to the CEO on matters relating to SchoolCater. As manager, she takes responsibility for SchoolCater’s Product development, marketing, and customer relations. Under her, adoption of SchoolCater has doubled in Abakaliki and it’s environs. Convinced that her product promises a great deal to educational institutions, she dreams of having it adopted throughout Nigeria and beyond. A lover of God and of good food, she is a historian and an expert in International Relations. Before joining Listacc, she had marketed products for some other companies.

                   

Nneka Ogbonna

Head of Finance

Nneka takes care of Listacc’s finances. As Head of Finance, she keeps the company’s books. She also works closely with the CEO as a member of the Administrative department of the company in allocating funds for various needs. Over the years, Nneka has kept money for every single group and organisation she has been a part of since her teenage years.
